From April 17, 2026, to July 12, 2026, I completed my internship program at Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd. This was a valuable opportunity for me to access and accumulate in-depth knowledge of cloud computing through self-study, combined with technology sharing sessions from experts and organizers. Here, my team and I successfully developed an automated job posting aggregation platform integrated with AI to assess the compatibility of CVs with job requirements.

Specific contributions to the project:

**Design & Deployment:** Participated with the team from the ideation and architectural design phases to product implementation.

**Serverless Development:** Built the basic serverless structure using AWS SAM and managed system resources via AWS CLI.

**Automated Data Processing:** Configured EventBridge Schedule combined with Lambda for automated web scraping; applied SQS as a queue system to buffer and optimize job processing performance.

**Extraction & Storage:** Used AWS Textract to digitize PDF data into text, and designed and managed the database on DynamoDB.

**Hosting:** Deployed and released the web application through AWS Amplify.

Acquired Skills:
The hands-on work experience has significantly enhanced my programming mindset and serverless architectural design capabilities, as well as my proficiency in operating services on the AWS ecosystem. Seamlessly connecting services (Lambda, SQS, EventBridge, Textract) into an automated pipeline helped me optimize my problem-solving skills. Concurrently, the collaborative work environment helped me refine my communication, teamwork, and professional technical writing skills.

Work Ethics:
I always strictly adhered to office hours, fully participated in events, complied with organizational regulations, and proactively interacted with team members to accelerate work progress.
