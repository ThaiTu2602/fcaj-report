> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working atmosphere at FCJ is very open, friendly, and without any gap between members. The office space is scientifically arranged and organized, fostering inspiration and high concentration while working. In particular, the members here are always willing to accompany and support each other in solving issues, even outside working hours.

**2. Support from Mentors / Team Admin**  
The management board and mentors play an extremely important role in shaping the mindset and orientation for interns. Whenever I encounter a difficult problem or get stuck technically, I always receive prompt, enthusiastic feedback along with very valuable guidance from them.

**3. Alignment between Work and Academic Major**  
The journey at the bootcamp has helped me consolidate and expand my in-depth knowledge of Cloud Computing. These practical experiences are completely aligned with and strongly support my career direction to become a Backend Developer.

**4. Opportunities for Knowledge Accumulation & Personal Skill Development**  
The program brings immense learning value, highlighted by the technology event series held periodically on group Saturdays. This is a precious opportunity for me to listen to real-world lessons, hands-on experiences, and career advice from experienced experts.

**5. Organizational Culture & Team Spirit**  
The community of interns in this cohort has a high spirit of solidarity. Everyone shows friendliness, active interaction, and doesn't hesitate to share and help each other overcome difficulties throughout the project implementation.

**6. Benefits & Policies for Interns**  
The organization always creates the best conditions for students with a flexible time management mechanism, helping to balance studying and working. Besides, the opportunity to directly participate in high-quality internal training programs is an exceptional privilege.

### Additional Questions

* **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
  It is the ideal working environment without pressure, along with the dedicated guidance and companionship from the Admin team.

* **What do you think the organization needs to optimize for future interns?**  
  I hope that the time or frequency of working directly at the office will be increased even more.

* **Would you be willing to recommend this place to your friends? Why?**  
  Definitely yes. For those who are planning to pursue DevOps, the Cloud ecosystem, or want to master AWS technology solutions, this is an extremely suitable launchpad.

### Suggestions & Expectations

* **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**  
  I suggest the organizers could integrate some short case studies (Mini-tasks) into the roadmap. This would not only help teams improve their reflexes and problem-solving mindset but also provide a great opportunity to get hands-on experience with core AWS services.
