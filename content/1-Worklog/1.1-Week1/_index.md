### Week 1 Objectives:

* Participate in the AWS Kickoff Event of the internship program.
* Read and understand the general rules and regulations of the internship organization.
* Initialize a personal AWS account for learning and hands-on practice.

### Week 1 Achievements:

* **Completed the Kickoff Event**: Fully participated in the kickoff ceremony, understood the 12-week training roadmap and the objectives to be achieved during the internship program.
* **Mastered Internship Organization Rules**: Read and clearly understood the regulations regarding working hours, reporting schedules, workflows, and evaluation criteria at the internship organization.
* **Successfully Initialized AWS Account**: Registered and configured a personal AWS account, set up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to secure the account.
* **Familiarized with AWS Management Console**: Practiced navigating the AWS management interface, understood how to search and access core services on the Cloud platform.
* **Built Initial Cloud Knowledge Foundation**: Gained an overall understanding of the AWS service ecosystem and the role of each service category in Cloud architecture.
